Projects

Here are some of the projects I've worked on:

Servlist

Servlist was a two-week full-stack web application project created as part of the requirements for completion of the ALX Software Engineering program. The servlist platform was meant for small business owners to create a comprehensive profile of their goods and services, thus offering them greater visibility.
Development technologies: Django, SQLite, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript.
Hosting and serving: render, Gunicorn.
The project is available at servlist.onrender.com.
However, due to competing priorities by team members, the project is no longer under development.

ServiceMtaani

ServiceMtaani was a two-week full-stack web application project created as part of the requirements for completion of the ALX Software Engineering program. The ServiceMtaani platform was meant to connect auto-mechanics, parts sellers, and car owners. Car owners could list repair jobs on the platform, auto-mechanics could bid for the jobs, and parts sellers could list parts for sale on the platform.
Development technologies: Flask, MySQL, HTML, CSS, jQuery, Bootstrap.
Hosting and serving: Google Cloud, Nginx, Gunicorn.
The project was accessible at servicemtaani.me for about three months, after which the Google Cloud free trial period expired. Due to competing priorities by team members, the project is no longer under development.
